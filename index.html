About IntroForge

IntroForge makes networking easier by automating cold outreach. We help students and young professionals identify mutual connections, craft personalized messages, and manage follow-ups, so they can build meaningful networks with less effort. Whether you're reaching out to alumni, recruiters, or mentors, IntroForge ensures your outreach is relevant, organized, and effective—so you don’t miss out on valuable career-building opportunities.

Our mission is simple: To empower students and young professionals to build meaningful networks through optimized cold outreach, so they can unlock career opportunities early and confidently. We started IntroForge because we've experienced the challenges of cold outreach firsthand, and we knew there had to be a better way.

We envision a world where every student, regardless of background or network, can access opportunities through intentional and effective connections.

Key Features


LinkedIn & Resume Integration


IntroForge seamlessly integrates with LinkedIn and personal resumes to identify relevant contacts and mutual connections. It highlights shared experiences, educational backgrounds, and professional overlaps, giving users actionable insights on how to reach out.

Tailored Messages


Increases students' chances of getting responses by ensuring their emails are relevant, professional, and thoughtfully tailored to each recipient.


Outreach Organizer with Notes & Follow-ups


IntroForge helps users stay on top of their networking efforts by providing a space to log notes for each conversation. Ensures students stay consistent and organized, allowing them to follow up effectively and build long-term relationships without losing track of key interactions.

The Problem We're Solving

The Problem

  • Time-Consuming Cold Outreach: Identifying mutual connections, crafting forwardable emails, and securing introductions takes significant time and effort.
  • Lack of Student-Focused Networking Tools: Professional tools like Apollo.io exist, but students lack platforms tailored to their unique networking needs.
  • Missed Opportunities: Without effective outreach strategies, students often fail to connect with professionals early, missing out on valuable career-building experiences.

Our Solution

  • Optimized Cold Outreach: Automates email personalization, helping users identify mutual connections and structure forwardable emails.
  • Networking as a Resource: A reliable platform for students to connect with mentors, recruiters, and alumni—not just for job opportunities but for meaningful engagement.
  • Scaling Connections: With a phased approach, we start by serving students and then expand to support young professionals and start-up founders.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How is IntroForge different from LinkedIn?

A: IntroForge focuses on quality over quantity by helping users build more meaningful relationships through targeted outreach. Our platform highlights mutual connections, shared experiences, and relevant overlaps, making it easier to initiate authentic conversations. Unlike LinkedIn, which is a broad professional network, IntroForge provides tools specifically designed to enhance cold outreach and networking efforts in a more personalized and organized way.

Q: Is my data safe with IntroForge?

A: Yes. We prioritize security by using OAuth authentication and ensuring full GDPR compliance. Your data is encrypted, and we only collect the minimum information needed to deliver a seamless experience.

Q: Is IntroForge free?

A: Yes! IntroForge is completely free during our pilot phase and is currently available only to students. In the future, we may introduce premium features and expand access to professionals and founders, but the core platform will always remain free for students.

Q: Do I need any networking experience to use IntroForge?

A: Not at all! IntroForge is user-friendly and easy to navigate, making it accessible for anyone looking to grow their network—whether you're just starting out or already experienced with outreach.

Q: How does IntroForge save time on networking?

A: IntroForge helps users stay organized and consistent by highlighting mutual connections and shared experiences, providing templates for tailored outreach, and tracking follow-ups. Instead of manually juggling contacts and messages, users can focus on building authentic relationships, knowing that their networking efforts are well-organized and efficient.
